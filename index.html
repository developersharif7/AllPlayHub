Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Effective Date: July 16, 2025

Welcome to AllPlayHub — a secure and privacy-respecting platform for uploading, playing, and discovering HTML5 games and apps. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete transparency about how your data is handled — or more accurately, how it is not handled, because we simply don't collect any personal data at all.

🔐 1. No Personal Data Collection

  • We do not ask for account registration or login.
  • We do not request or store names, emails, phone numbers, or passwords.
  • We do not collect IP addresses, location data, or device info.
  • We do not use cookies or third-party analytics.

💾 2. Your Data Stays With You

Any content you interact with, such as uploads, drafts, or comment activity, is saved locally in your browser via secure client-side storage like localStorage.

  • This data never reaches our servers.
  • Only you can view or manage your saved items.
  • Data will reset if you clear your browser cache or switch devices.

📤 3. Upload Review System

All uploads are directly submitted to the admin for review. You can upload your games or tools with:

  • A project title
  • A thumbnail/logo
  • Description and your HTML5 game or app code

Note: No personal info is attached or required. Admin approval is manual, and content may be rejected for inappropriate or harmful material.

🛡️ 4. Data Protection Philosophy

We believe in the digital right to privacy. Our platform is designed to operate with zero user tracking, zero cookies, and zero behavioral profiling. You are not watched, analyzed, or followed. What you do on AllPlayHub is your business — not ours.

📱 5. Mobile-Friendly & Secure

This website is optimized for all devices. Whether you're uploading a game or playing one, your experience is safe, responsive, and anonymous.
